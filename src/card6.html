Managed Endpoint Protection

Description

This service focuses on securing endpoints (computers, mobile devices) within an organization's network against malware, ransomware, and other cyber threats. It involves deploying and managing advanced endpoint security solutions that include antivirus, anti-malware, and endpoint detection and response (EDR) capabilities. Additionally, it includes continuous monitoring, threat hunting, and incident response to detect and mitigate threats effectively.

Benefits: Enhanced endpoint security, reduced risk of malware infections, improved incident detection and response, and protection against zero-day attacks.
