Security Information and Event Management (SIEM) Services

Description

This service revolves around implementing and managing SIEM solutions that aggregate and analyze log data from various sources across the organization's IT infrastructure. It involves real-time monitoring, correlation of security events, and alerting on suspicious activities or potential security incidents. Additionally, it includes incident investigation, forensic analysis, and compliance reporting to help organizations meet regulatory requirements.

Benefits: Centralized visibility into security events, early detection of threats, improved incident response, compliance management, and actionable insights for security teams.
