Continuous Data Protection (CDP) Solutions

Description

This service offers continuous backup and real-time replication of data to ensure near-zero recovery point objectives (RPOs) and recovery time objectives (RTOs). It involves capturing every change made to data and storing it immediately to enable granular recovery points and quick data restoration. Additionally, it includes monitoring and alerting on backup status, ensuring data consistency, and optimizing bandwidth usage for replication.

Benefits: Minimal data loss, fast recovery times, granular recovery options, real-time data protection, and enhanced data integrity and availability.
